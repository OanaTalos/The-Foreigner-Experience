"Who are these people??"

We are an NGO (Non-Governmental Organisation) and all our services are FREE. But... nothing is really for free, is it now? We can imagine you thinking that. And you are right.
Which is why you see a shopping cart on this website. All are services come in exchange for smile. Yes, you read it well: smiles.
The more smiles you give us, the more services we provide you.
We believe in the soul healing power of good mood. And what else than smiles to bring that?
Sounds weird? Add to cart the experiences you want to join and come meet us. You will see we keep count of your smiles and apply fines if the smile payment has not been made.
